About JNTUK

Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK), located in Andhra Pradesh, is a prestigious institution known for its excellence in technical education. Established in 1946, it has grown into a premier university offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines of engineering, technology, and sciences.

JNTUK is renowned for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, advanced laboratories, and an extensive network of affiliated colleges across Andhra Pradesh. The university is committed to fostering academic research and innovation through collaborations with industries and research organizations.

With a rich history and a vision for the future, JNTUK emphasizes holistic education that equips students with technical expertise and leadership skills to succeed in a competitive global environment. The university is a beacon of learning, nurturing talented minds for national and global progress.

The institution also provides opportunities for extracurricular growth and promotes community engagement, ensuring students receive a comprehensive educational experience.
