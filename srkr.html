About Sagi Rama Krishnam Raju Engineering College (SRKR)

Sagi Rama Krishnam Raju Engineering College (SRKR), established in 1980, is a prestigious engineering institution located in Bhimavaram, Andhra Pradesh. Known for its strong academic programs and emphasis on research, SRKR is a leader in engineering education in the region.

Affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK), and approved by AICTE, SRKR offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across engineering disciplines, including Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, Civil, and Electrical Engineering. The college has a reputation for its quality teaching and experienced faculty.

SRKR provides state-of-the-art laboratories, innovation hubs, and research centers to foster a collaborative and innovative learning environment. It also hosts an Institution Innovation Council (IIC) and an Entrepreneurship Development Cell to encourage entrepreneurial initiatives among students.

With a rich history of excellence in engineering education, SRKR Engineering College continues to play a pivotal role in shaping skilled professionals who are well-prepared for successful careers in the industry.
