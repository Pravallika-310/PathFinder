About Andhra University

Andhra University, located in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, is one of India's oldest and most prestigious educational institutions. Established in 1926, the university has a rich legacy of academic excellence and is a hub for quality education in diverse fields.

The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines, including arts, science, engineering, law, and management. Andhra University is known for its sprawling campus, well-equipped research centers, and dedicated faculty who contribute to the holistic development of students.

With a strong emphasis on research and innovation, the university collaborates with industries and organizations to ensure students are prepared for real-world challenges. The vibrant campus life, state-of-the-art facilities, and emphasis on extracurricular activities make Andhra University a sought-after destination for students from across the country.

As a pioneer in education and research, Andhra University continues to uphold its mission of providing accessible and affordable education to empower individuals and contribute to societal progress.
