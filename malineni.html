About Malineni Lakshmaiah Engineering College

Malineni Lakshmaiah Engineering College, established in 1999, is located in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh. Founded by Malineni Perumallu Educational Society, the college is known for its commitment to quality education in the field of engineering and technology.

Affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK), and approved by AICTE, the college offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate courses across engineering disciplines, including Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, Civil, and Electrical Engineering. The institution places a strong emphasis on hands-on learning and industry-relevant training.

Malineni Lakshmaiah Engineering College has well-equipped laboratories and research facilities to encourage student innovation. The college also has an active Entrepreneurship Development Cell that supports students in developing entrepreneurial skills and pursuing startup ideas.

With a focus on skill development and career readiness, Malineni Lakshmaiah Engineering College provides an environment that promotes both academic and personal growth, preparing students for successful careers in the industry.
