About NRI Institute of Technology

NRI Institute of Technology, located in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, was established to provide quality technical education and foster innovation among its students. The college has become a leading institution, offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, and technology.

Approved by AICTE and affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK), the institution is known for its academic excellence and emphasis on research and industry-oriented education. The campus provides state-of-the-art laboratories, libraries, and facilities for holistic learning.

NRI Institute of Technology actively promotes entrepreneurship and innovation through its dedicated innovation centers and collaboration with industry leaders. The institution also focuses on nurturing leadership skills and ethics, preparing students to excel in their chosen fields.

The college's commitment to excellence is reflected in its placement track record, research achievements, and focus on empowering students with technical and soft skills.
