About Chalapathi Engineering College

Chalapathi Engineering College, located in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, is a renowned institution dedicated to providing quality technical education. Established with a mission to empower students through knowledge and skills, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology.

The institution is approved by AICTE and affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK). Chalapathi Engineering College is known for its well-equipped campus, advanced laboratories, and a faculty committed to academic excellence and innovation.

The college emphasizes practical learning and industry collaboration to prepare students for the competitive global job market. With a focus on research, innovation, and entrepreneurship, Chalapathi Engineering College fosters an environment of growth and exploration.

The institution is also committed to holistic student development by encouraging participation in extracurricular activities, community engagement, and leadership initiatives, making it a preferred destination for aspiring engineers.
