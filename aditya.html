About Aditya Engineering College

Aditya Engineering College, established in 2001, is a prestigious institution located in Surampalem, Andhra Pradesh. Part of the Aditya Group of Institutions, the college is renowned for its commitment to academic excellence and innovation in engineering education.

Affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK), and approved by AICTE, Aditya Engineering College offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across engineering disciplines, including Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, Civil, and Electrical Engineering. The college is known for its advanced infrastructure and focus on practical learning.

Aditya Engineering College has modern laboratories, research centers, and collaborative partnerships with industry leaders to foster innovation and entrepreneurship among students. The college also hosts an Institution Innovation Council (IIC) and an Entrepreneurship Development Cell to encourage entrepreneurial spirit.

With its strong emphasis on industry-aligned curriculum, Aditya Engineering College prepares students for successful careers and makes a significant impact on the engineering and technology sectors.
