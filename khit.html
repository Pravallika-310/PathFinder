About Kallam Haranadha Reddy Institute of Technology

Kallam Haranadha Reddy Institute of Technology (KHIT), established in 2010, is located in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh. Known for its dedication to providing high-quality technical education, KHIT has grown into a well-regarded institution in the region.

Affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK), and approved by AICTE, KHIT offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering fields, including Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, Civil, and Electrical Engineering. The institution is committed to fostering a strong foundation in technical skills and innovation.

KHIT provides its students with modern laboratories, research facilities, and a vibrant learning environment. The college has an active Institution Innovation Council (IIC) and an Entrepreneurship Development Cell to support students interested in innovation and startups.

KHIT emphasizes a well-rounded education that combines academic rigor with professional skill development, preparing students for success in their careers and contributing to society.
