About Narasaraopeta Engineering College

Narasaraopeta Engineering College (NEC), established in 1998, is located in Narasaraopet, Guntur District, Andhra Pradesh. NEC is known for its dedication to providing high-quality education in engineering and technology, making it one of the leading institutions in the area.

The college is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK), and approved by AICTE, New Delhi. Accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for several programs, NEC offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in branches like CSE, ECE, Mechanical, Civil, and EEE.

NEC is committed to fostering innovation and research, with state-of-the-art facilities and an Institution Innovation Council (IIC). The college encourages students to participate in skill development programs, internships, and entrepreneurial activities through its Entrepreneurship Development Cell.

With a strong focus on industry connections, NEC provides an environment that helps students prepare for successful careers by equipping them with both technical knowledge and soft skills.
