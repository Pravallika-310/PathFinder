About KITS Akshar College

KITS Akshar College, located in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, is dedicated to providing high-quality technical education and fostering innovation among its students. Known for its student-centered approach and academic rigor, KITS Akshar offers undergraduate programs in various engineering disciplines.

The college is approved by AICTE and affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK). The campus boasts modern infrastructure, advanced laboratories, and a vibrant learning environment aimed at equipping students with the skills needed to succeed in their careers.

KITS Akshar College emphasizes practical learning and industry exposure, with initiatives to promote research, entrepreneurship, and innovation. The college supports a strong culture of mentorship, helping students grow into capable professionals with leadership qualities and ethical values.

With a focus on holistic development, KITS Akshar College is committed to empowering students with technical expertise, soft skills, and a passion for lifelong learning.
