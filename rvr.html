About RVR & JC College of Engineering

RVR & JC College of Engineering, established in 1985, is located in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh. It is one of the premier engineering institutions in the region, known for its commitment to providing quality technical education and fostering a spirit of innovation among students.

The college is affiliated with Acharya Nagarjuna University and approved by AICTE, New Delhi. It has earned NAAC accreditation with an "A" grade and is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for various undergraduate programs. The institution offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across disciplines like CSE, ECE, Mechanical, Civil, and IT.

RVR & JC College is dedicated to promoting research and development, with centers for innovation and skill development. The college has collaborations with various industries and universities to enhance students' learning experience and practical exposure. It also hosts an Entrepreneurship Development Cell that supports students in developing business ideas and ventures.

The campus is equipped with modern amenities and facilities to provide a comprehensive learning environment, nurturing both academic and professional growth among students.
