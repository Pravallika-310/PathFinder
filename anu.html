About Acharya Nagarjuna University

Acharya Nagarjuna University (ANU), located in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, is a prominent educational institution named after the great Buddhist philosopher Nagarjuna. Established in 1976, the university is known for its commitment to excellence in education and research.

ANU offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields, including arts, science, commerce, law, and technology. The university has a sprawling campus equipped with modern amenities, advanced research facilities, and a vibrant academic environment.

Acharya Nagarjuna University fosters innovation and collaboration, preparing students to face the challenges of a dynamic global landscape. The university has strong ties with industries and research organizations to provide practical exposure and career opportunities to its students.

Besides academics, ANU encourages participation in cultural, sports, and extracurricular activities, ensuring a holistic development experience for its students.
