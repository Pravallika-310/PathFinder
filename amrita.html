About Amrita University, Amaravati

Amrita University, one of India’s top-ranked private universities, is expanding to Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh. Known for its commitment to high-quality education and cutting-edge research, Amrita provides a wide array of programs, including engineering, medicine, business, and social sciences, while focusing on societal impact.

With an emphasis on holistic development, Amrita Amaravati will offer state-of-the-art facilities, advanced labs, and collaboration opportunities with leading institutions globally. Amrita is particularly known for its humanitarian outlook and integration of values-based education, fostering a culture of innovation, compassion, and integrity among students.

The campus aims to contribute to local and global advancements by supporting a vibrant research environment and encouraging entrepreneurship and sustainability initiatives. Amrita’s Amaravati campus is dedicated to creating leaders and innovators ready to address tomorrow’s challenges.
