About Vasireddy Venkatadri Institute of Technology

Vasireddy Venkatadri Institute of Technology (VVIT), established in 2007, is located in Nambur, Guntur District, Andhra Pradesh. Known for its academic excellence and commitment to innovation, VVIT is a top-choice engineering college in the region.

Affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK), VVIT is approved by AICTE and accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for multiple programs. The college also holds an “A” grade accreditation from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), underscoring its quality education and facilities.

VVIT provides a conducive environment for innovation and entrepreneurship through initiatives like the Institution Innovation Council (IIC), Center of Excellence, and various industry partnerships. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines, including CSE, ECE, Mechanical, Civil, and IT.

VVIT supports students in transforming innovative ideas into viable businesses through its Entrepreneurial Development Cell, providing mentoring, funding opportunities, and exposure to industry connections.
