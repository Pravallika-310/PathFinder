About Tirumala Engineering College

Tirumala Engineering College, established in 2008, is situated in Narasaraopet, Guntur District, Andhra Pradesh. The college is committed to providing quality education in engineering and technology and is one of the recognized institutions in the region.

Affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK), and approved by AICTE, the college offers undergraduate programs in several engineering disciplines, including CSE, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering. The institution emphasizes practical learning and industry engagement to equip students with skills for professional success.

Tirumala Engineering College fosters a culture of innovation and research through dedicated centers and labs. It provides students with the resources and mentorship needed for academic and professional development. The college also has an Entrepreneurship Development Cell that helps students pursue entrepreneurial endeavors.
